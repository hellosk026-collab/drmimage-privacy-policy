DRM Image 4K Engine

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how DRM Image 4K Engine handles information when you use the desktop and mobile versions of the app.

Effective date: March 14, 2026

1. What The App Does

DRM Image 4K Engine is a local-first creative app that helps you generate and edit images and videos using prompts, reference images, and your own API access to third-party AI services.

2. Information You Provide

The app may process the following information that you choose to provide:

  • Your Gemini API key.
  • Images, videos, prompts, and reference files that you upload or create in the app.
  • Configuration choices such as subject settings, prompts, history selections, and export actions.

3. API Keys

You are responsible for supplying your own Gemini API key.

  • On Windows, the app stores your API key using Windows Credential Manager.
  • On supported mobile platforms, the app stores your API key using platform secure storage.
  • The app is designed not to store your API key in plain text workspace files during normal use.

4. Images, Videos, And Prompts

Your source images, prompts, reference content, and generated outputs are primarily handled on your device unless and until you choose to send a generation request.

When you trigger image or video generation, relevant prompts and selected media may be transmitted to Google's generative AI services so the request can be processed.

5. Third-Party Services And Network Requests

The app may connect to third-party services or resources, including:

  • Google generative AI endpoints for generation and API key validation.
  • Third-party web assets used by the current app shell, such as Tailwind CDN and Google Fonts.

Those services may receive technical request data such as your IP address, device or browser metadata, and request timing information as part of normal network communication.

6. Local Storage And History

The app may keep local history or generated output references on your device to support app features such as session history, previews, downloads, and editing workflows.

Saved files remain under your control on your device unless you choose to export, share, or upload them elsewhere.

7. What We Do Not Intend To Collect

Based on the current app design, DRM Image 4K Engine does not include:

  • User account registration.
  • A DRM Image 4K Engine-hosted cloud account system for storing your projects.
  • Built-in analytics dashboards or advertising SDKs controlled by us.

If this changes in a future release, this policy should be updated before those features are enabled.

8. Mobile Permissions

On Android or other supported mobile platforms, the app may request access needed for features such as file selection, local file saving, or secure key storage. Those permissions are used to operate the app features you choose to use.

9. Data Retention And Deletion

  • You can remove your stored API key from within the app.
  • You can delete local files and generated content from your device at any time.
  • Uninstalling the app may remove app-local data, but files you explicitly exported may remain where you saved them.

10. Children

The app is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law to consent to data processing for this type of service.

11. Changes To This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect app changes, legal requirements, or platform changes. The effective date at the top indicates the latest revision.

12. Contact

For privacy or support questions, contact: support@drmimage4k.com
